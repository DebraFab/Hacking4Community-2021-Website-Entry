What To Know For:

For Educators

Educators are often the first to notice mental health problems. Here are some ways you can help students and their families.Consult with a school counselor, nurse, or administrator and the student's parents if you observe one or more of the following behaviors:

Feeling very sad or withdrawn for more than two weeks

Seriously trying to harm oneself, or making plans to do so

Sudden overwhelming fear for no reason, sometimes with a racing heart or fast breathing

Involvement in many fights or desire to badly hurt others

Severe out-of-control behavior that can hurt oneself or others
